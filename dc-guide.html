Neighborhoods to Explore

Georgetown

Historic neighborhood with cobblestone streets, upscale shopping, great restaurants, and the Georgetown University campus.

Adams Morgan

Eclectic neighborhood known for diverse dining, vibrant nightlife, and unique shops.

Dupont Circle

Bustling area with restaurants, bars, bookstores, and a great farmers market on Sundays.

U Street Corridor

Historic neighborhood with rich music history, trendy bars, and excellent restaurants.

Capitol Hill

Home to the Capitol Building, with charming row houses, Eastern Market, and great restaurants.

The Wharf

Revitalized waterfront area with restaurants, bars, shops, and beautiful water views.

Travel Tips

  • Getting Around: DC's Metro system is excellent for getting around. Consider getting a SmarTrip card!
  • Free Museums: All Smithsonian museums are free, but some require timed entry passes - reserve in advance!
  • Walking: DC is very walkable. Many monuments are within walking distance of each other on the National Mall.
